# UI Design Rules: Modern Psychological & Editorial Style

## 1. Design Philosophy & Direction
*   **Core Concept**: **Demystification**. Reframe "fortune-telling/mysticism" into **"psychological analysis"** or **"self-exploration data reports"**.
*   **Reference Benchmarks**: **The Pattern**, **Co-Star**, **CHANI**.
*   **Target Audience**: Gen Z and Urban Professionals who value **Self-Healing**, **Mindfulness**, and **Psychological Insight**.
*   **Key Vibe**: Scientific, Credible, Premium, Quiet Luxury.

## 2. Visual Language (The "Anti-Mystic" Aesthetic)
### ❌ STRICTLY AVOID
*   **Cliché Symbols**: Crystal balls, magic circles, literal zodiac wheels, heavy tarot textures, witch hats.
*   **Cheap Effects**: Generic "galaxy" backgrounds, neon strobes, heavy skeuomorphism.
*   **Dark Gothic**: Avoid the "dark witchy" look unless specifically requested.

### ✅ MANDATORY ELEMENTS
*   **Color Palette (Warm Neutrals)**:
    *   **Backgrounds**: Use **Earth Tones**: Beige, Cream, Bone, Off-White (e.g., `#F5F5F0`, `#F2EEE9`). **Avoid sterile pure white.**
    *   **Typography**: Stark Black (`#000000`) or Deep Charcoal.
    *   **Accents**: Use **Blurry Aura Gradients** (Soft mesh gradients) to convey mood/energy.
*   **Typography (Editorial/Magazine Style)**:
    *   **Style**: **Swiss Design Style**. Grid-based, heavy emphasis on readability.
    *   **Headers**: Big, Bold, Sans-Serif fonts (like Inter, Helvetica Now). Text is the main visual element.
*   **UI Components**:
    *   **Cards**: Flat functionality. Subtle color variations. **Large Corner Radius** (16px-24px).
    *   **Buttons**: **Pill-shaped / Capsule**. Solid black background with white text.
    *   **Icons**: **Ultra-thin lines**. Minimalist, abstract.

## 3. Design Persona
> "Design this application as if it were a high-end fashion magazine or a modern mental health tool, not a traditional horoscope app. Prioritize negative space, bold typography, and warm, organic textures."
